Privacy Policy – JROTC Guidon

Effective date: July 16, 2026

JROTC Guidon is a reference and study tool for JROTC cadets. This policy explains what information the app uses and how.

Information We Collect

The app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All preferences — including ribbon rack selections, theme, quiz progress, and premium status — are saved locally on your device and are never sent anywhere.

In-App Purchases

The app offers an optional one-time premium upgrade. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We never receive or store your payment information.

Network Requests

The app loads certain photos of historical figures and chain of command officials from Wikimedia Commons (wikimedia.org). These requests go directly from your device to Wikimedia's servers. We do not log or intercept them. Wikimedia's privacy policy governs those connections.

The app may prompt you to leave a rating through Apple's or Google's built-in review system. This is handled entirely by your device's operating system.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.
